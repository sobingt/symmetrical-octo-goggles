Add OfficeR&D action

When choosing the action for your new Zap, search for 'OfficeR&D'.

While setting up your Zap you will be asked for your OfficeR&D credentials. Just type in the ones you use to log in at OfficeR&D.

Connect OfficeR&D with Stripe

Get your Stripe keys

  1. If you don't have Stripe account go to https://stripe.com/ and create one
  2. Open API Keys under your Account Settings in the Stripe dashboard (or click here)
  3. You will see two pairs of keys - test and live ones
  4. Go to our payments gateway help section for more details
